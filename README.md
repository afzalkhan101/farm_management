# 🌾 Farm Management Module for Odoo 18

A complete **Farm Management System** built on **Odoo 18** to help manage farm operations such as poultry, livestock, egg production, feeding schedules, and expenses.

---

## 🚀 Features
- 🐓 Poultry and livestock management  
- 🥚 Egg production tracking (small, medium, large, double yolk, broken, etc.)  
- 🍼 Feeding & vaccination schedule management  
- 💰 Expense and revenue tracking  
- 📊 Reports & dashboard for decision-making  
- 🔒 User-based access rights  
- ✅ Easy to customize and extend
